Child Stunting Awareness Campaign

Child stunting is a serious issue in Malaysia. Despite being an upper middle-income country, Malaysia faces high rates of child stunting, comparable to conflict-stricken countries like Iraq and Palestine.

Statistics reveal that X% of children under the age of 5 in Malaysia are stunted, leading to long-term consequences such as impaired cognitive development and reduced earning potential.

Real-life stories from affected families shed light on the challenges they face and the importance of addressing this issue urgently.
